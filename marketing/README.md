# Marketing Strategy & Video Content Production

## Overview

This directory contains the marketing strategies for the **Autonomous App Studio** app portfolio. The video editor is the production tool that brings these strategies to life — producing ad creatives, social media clips, demo videos, and promotional content described in the marketing plans.

### Apps

| App | Description | Target Audience |
|-----|-------------|-----------------|
| **Park-It** | Premium monochrome parking app (iOS) | Urban drivers, commuters, city explorers |
| **Surve** | Survey and feedback app (iOS) | Teams, organizations, event planners |
| **Nutrio+** | Nutrition tracking app (iOS) | Health-conscious individuals |

- Content creation templates and UGC strategy
- Budget tiers: organic ($39/mo) → boosted ($239-439/mo) → paid ($639-1,639/mo)

## How the Video Editor Connects to Marketing Strategy

### Content Calendar Execution
The social media content calendars in both strategy documents specify video content (Reels, TikToks, demos) multiple times per week. The video editor is the production tool that creates these assets using the appropriate app preset + platform preset.

### Ad Creative Production
The paid user acquisition plans specify video ad variants (UGC-style, feature demos, pain-point videos) for Meta Ads and TikTok Ads. The video editor produces these with consistent branding using `park-it-ad` and `surve-ad` presets.

### Social Media Clips
Cross-promotion campaigns ("Smart City Challenge", "City Hacks", joint influencer content) require short-form video content. The video editor handles aspect ratios, caption overlays, and CTA cards for each platform.

### Production Workflow
1. Check `video-content-brief.md` for the next video in the production queue
2. Select the appropriate preset in the video editor
3. Follow the shot list / script spec
4. Export with the specified aspect ratio and caption style
5. Deliver to social media scheduling tool (Buffer/Later)
